## What VestingWatch Tracks

### Cliff Events
Large, one-time unlocks that flood supply. The highest dump risk.

Example: 25% of total supply vesting in 7 days → massive sell pressure incoming.

### Linear Vesting
Gradual unlock schedules. Lower risk per day, but compounding supply increase.

Example: 1M tokens/month for 24 months → steady sell pressure over time.

### Team & Investor Allocations
Who's getting unlocked tokens? VCs with 100x gains or team members with long-term skin in the game?
